    Health Tips

    • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
    • Incorporate a variety of fruits and vegetables into your diet.
    • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy weight and improve overall health.
    • Limit processed foods and added sugars in your diet.
    • Prioritize whole grains over refined grains.

    Good Foods for Your Diet

    • Leafy Greens: Spinach, kale, and Swiss chard are low in calories but rich in nutrients.
    • Lean Proteins: Chicken breast, turkey, and fish provide essential protein without excess fat.
    • Whole Grains: Oats, quinoa, and brown rice are great sources of fiber and energy.
    • Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, and chia seeds are packed with healthy fats and protein.
    • Fruits: Berries, apples, and bananas are high in fiber and vitamins.
